Events of the Past...

Trunk or Treat!

Trunk or Treat is a family-friendly Halloween tradition that provides a safe and fun alternative to traditional trick-or-treating. Last year the Galway municipal center hosted this event where participants decorated their vehicle trunks in various Halloween themes and filled them with candies and other treats. Children went from one creatively decorated trunk to another collecting sweets and enjoying the festive atmosphere. Apt for all ages; non-food trinket options are available.


Spooky Soirée

This "Spooky Soirée" promises an evening of mysterious enchantment and Halloween thrills, offering an elegant twist to the traditional haunted festivities. Guests are invited to don their finest costumes and enter a world of eerie elegance, where ghostly decor and hauntingly good music set the stage for a night of spooky fun. With gourmet treats and devilish delights, this event ensures an unforgettable and sophisticated Halloween celebration! Apt for adults 18 and over.


Haunted Storytime

Get ready for a spooktacular adventure as children gather for 'Haunted Storytime.' This enchanting event brings the magic of Halloween to life with captivating tales, friendly ghouls, and interactive storytelling, creating a safe and thrilling experience for young imaginations. Embark on a journey filled with laughter, surprises, and the enchantment of Halloween night! Join us if you dare...
